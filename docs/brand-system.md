# Micirql brand system

## Positioning

Micirql is a founder-led Android software company building a portfolio of focused products around real operational problems. CapDent is the flagship live product, not the full definition of the company.

## Brand personality

- Confident and precise
- Technical without feeling cold
- Forward-looking without futuristic clichés
- Creative through structure, not decoration
- Suitable for a multi-product software company

## Logo concept

The Micirql mark combines two interlocking constructed forms. The shapes suggest:

- products being assembled in layers;
- forward movement;
- connection between workflow and software;
- one product creating the foundation for the next.

The amber node represents the moment an operational problem becomes a product opportunity.

The mark does not use a phone outline, Android mascot, Google Play triangle, circuit pattern or a generic letter A.

## Colour system

| Role | Colour | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Graphite | Primary company background and authority | `#0B0D12` |
| Electric indigo | Product engineering and primary action | `#6268FF` |
| Amber | Energy, opportunity and selective emphasis | `#FFB45B` |
| Warm white | High-contrast text and editorial surfaces | `#F5F3EE` |
| CapDent teal | Used only inside CapDent product contexts | `#087F72` |

Do not recolour the entire Micirql website teal. Teal is a product-level colour belonging to CapDent.

## Typography

The website uses platform-native variable display fonts where available:

- Display: Segoe UI Variable Display / Aptos Display
- Body: Aptos / Segoe UI Variable Text

The hierarchy relies on scale, spacing and weight rather than a generic rounded startup font.

## Usage principles

1. Keep the mark surrounded by clear space equal to at least one amber-node width.
2. Do not add gradients, glow effects, bevels or shadows to the mark.
3. Use the primary mark on dark or neutral backgrounds.
4. Use the black mark on white print applications.
5. Use the reversed mark on dark single-colour applications.
6. Keep CapDent branding visually distinct inside portfolio and product sections.
