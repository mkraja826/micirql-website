FAQ

Questions about the new pricing.

Is CapDent really free?

Yes. Every clinic-management feature is available without a feature subscription, and each clinic receives 1 GB of cloud storage.

What counts toward the 1 GB limit?

Uploaded files such as X-rays, prescriptions, reports, patient photographs, before-and-after images, profile pictures and clinic branding use the storage allowance.

What happens when my clinic reaches 1 GB?

New uploads pause until you delete files or choose a larger storage plan. Existing files remain accessible, and patient records, appointments, visits, payments, staff and reports continue working.

Do paid storage plans unlock extra features?

No. Free and paid clinics receive the same CapDent features. Paid plans increase only the total cloud-storage allowance.

Can I return to the free plan?

Yes. After reducing stored files below the free allowance, the clinic can return to the free plan at the end of the current billing period when subscriptions are available.

Does CapDent diagnose patients?

No. CapDent is clinic workflow and record-management software. Clinical decisions remain with qualified dental professionals.
