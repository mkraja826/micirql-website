FAQ

Questions clinics ask before switching.

What is CapDent?

CapDent is a dental clinic management app by Micirql. It organizes patient records, appointments, visits, fees, payments, prescriptions, X-rays, photos, reminders, staff workflows and clinic reports.

Is CapDent suitable for a small clinic?

Yes. The Free plan is positioned for a new single-owner clinic, while Professional and Clinic Intelligence plans support clinics that need more owner controls and analytics.

Which staff roles are supported?

CapDent includes role-specific application areas for the owner or head doctor, working doctors and receptionists.

Can CapDent store clinical images?

Yes. The app includes focused upload routes for prescriptions, X-rays and patient photos, keeping those files connected to the correct patient.

Does CapDent provide medical diagnosis?

No. It is clinic workflow and record-management software. Diagnosis and treatment decisions remain with qualified dental professionals.
