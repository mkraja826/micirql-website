FAQ

Questions clinics ask before switching.

What is CapDent?

CapDent is a dental clinic management app by Micirql for records, appointments, visits, payments, clinical uploads, reminders, staff and reports.

Is it suitable for a small clinic?

Yes. The Free plan is intended for a new single-owner clinic, with paid plans available as the clinic grows.

Which staff roles are supported?

Owner or head doctor, working doctor and receptionist workflows are supported.

Does CapDent diagnose patients?

No. CapDent is clinic workflow and record-management software. Clinical decisions remain with qualified dental professionals.
